Where do I change the daily video and description?

Just update the file /daily/latest.{lang}.json (see example below). The homepage will display it automatically.

Can I use TikTok instead of YouTube?

You can, but TikTok embeds sometimes fail due to third-party scripts/cookies policies. YouTube tends to be the most reliable for websites. Best setup: YouTube embed + TikTok link.
